# AngelSitting — Pricing model

AngelSitting is subscription-only, with three monthly plans billed via Stripe:
**Essential**, **Family**, and **Premium**.

- All plans are billed monthly and include the concierge service (matching, coordination,
  support, replacements).
- The babysitter's hourly wage for actual care hours is arranged separately and is not part
  of the subscription fee.
- Families can switch between plans (Essential ↔ Family ↔ Premium) at any time through the
  Stripe customer portal; the change applies from the next billing period.
- There is no setup fee and no registration fee — you only pay the monthly subscription once
  you choose a plan.

(The current price of each plan is maintained as a live fact in the brain, because prices
change over time.)
