# AngelSitting — Onboarding & booking flow

The journey for a new family, in order:

1. **Bewerbung (application)** — a short 3-step form: about you → about the children →
   contact details. This creates the family account.
2. **Familien-Brief (family brief)** — Malika sends a personalised welcome letter and a short
   matching questionnaire to understand the family's needs.
3. **Free 15-minute call with Malika** — a no-commitment intro call. This first consultation
   is free; families only commit once they choose a plan.
4. **Choose a subscription** — one of the three monthly plans (Essential, Family, Premium),
   billed monthly via Stripe.
5. **Matching & care** — Malika proposes curated babysitters; coordination and any
   replacements continue through the in-app chat.

Families can switch plans or cancel anytime through the Stripe customer portal.
