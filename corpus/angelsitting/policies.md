# AngelSitting — Policies

## Matching
Malika personally curates each proposal based on the family's needs, district, and schedule.
Families do not browse sitters; every match is concierge-curated.

## Billing & cancellation
- Subscriptions are monthly and managed through the Stripe customer portal.
- Families can change or cancel their plan themselves; cancellation takes effect at the end
  of the current billing period.

## Data & contact
- Families communicate with AngelSitting exclusively through Malika in the in-app chat.
- AngelSitting operates in Berlin and serves families across Berlin districts.
