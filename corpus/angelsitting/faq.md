# AngelSitting — FAQ

**Where does AngelSitting operate?**
Berlin only, matched per family by district (Bezirk), days, and hours.

**How do I get started?**
Submit a short application, receive a personalised family brief from Malika, then book a free
15-minute call. After the call, choose a monthly plan.

**Do I talk to the babysitters directly?**
During matching, no — Malika coordinates everything. You communicate with Malika in the
in-app chat.

**How are babysitters selected for me?**
Malika curates proposals by hand based on your needs, district, and schedule. There is no
self-service marketplace.

**Can I change or cancel my plan?**
Yes. You manage your subscription yourself through the Stripe customer portal. Changes apply
from the next billing period.

**Is this for last-minute babysitting?**
No. AngelSitting is for planned, recurring childcare, on a monthly subscription.

**What does the subscription include?**
The concierge service: matching, coordination, support, and replacements. The babysitter's
hourly wage for care hours is arranged separately.
