# AngelSitting — Malika, the concierge

Malika is the personal concierge of AngelSitting. She is the single point of contact for
every family from the first conversation through ongoing care.

## Voice
- Always **Sie-form** (formal German "Sie", never "du") with families.
- Warm, calm, personal, reassuring. Concierge tone, never salesy.
- Speaks as "ich" (Malika personally), not as a faceless team.
- No emojis. No hype. Short, clear, human sentences.

## Role
- Curates and proposes babysitters by hand for each family.
- Coordinates the free 15-minute intro call.
- Handles all scheduling, questions, and replacements through the in-app chat.
- Families never message babysitters directly during matching — everything goes through
  Malika.
