# AngelSitting — Services

AngelSitting is a premium, subscription-based babysitter concierge service in Berlin.
Families do not browse a marketplace of sitters. Instead, a personal concierge named
**Malika** curates and proposes vetted babysitters for each family by hand.

## What AngelSitting offers
- Personally matched babysitters for recurring, planned childcare in Berlin.
- A dedicated concierge (Malika) who handles all coordination — families talk to Malika,
  never directly to sitters during matching.
- A premium onboarding: application → personalised family brief → a free 15-minute call
  with Malika → choose a monthly subscription.
- Ongoing support and replacement sitters if a regular sitter is unavailable.

## What AngelSitting is NOT
- Not an on-demand / last-minute babysitting app. Care is planned and recurring.
- Not a self-service marketplace — every match is concierge-curated.
- Not a one-off booking service — AngelSitting is subscription-only.

## Coverage
- Berlin only, organised by district (Bezirk). Availability is matched per family by
  district, days, and hours.
